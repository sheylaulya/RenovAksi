# 🌊 RenovAksi: Coastal Village  
### *RenovAksi: Desa Pesisir Laut*

**RenovAksi** is a narrative-based **Life Simulation & Strategy game** that addresses the critical environmental crisis in Indonesia's coastal areas.  

Derived from the Indonesian words **Aksi** (Action) and **Renovasi** (Renovation), the name represents a direct call to action to rebuild regions on the brink of *extinction*.

---

## 📌 Background

Indonesia has the **2nd longest coastline in the world**, yet many coastal areas remain pockets of extreme poverty.

This project uses **Timbulsloko Village, Demak** as its primary case study — a village currently submerged by up to **2 meters of tidal flooding** and projected to disappear by **2030** without intervention.

---

## 🎮 Gameplay & Features

Players take on the role of a **KKN Student (Community Service Student)** tasked with helping villagers find adaptive solutions.

### 🌍 Core Systems

- **Exploration System**  
  Navigate through diverse zones, from the *Old Settlement* to the *Submerged Fishponds*.

- **Resource-Based Building**  
  Collect and manage materials like **Bamboo**, **Wood**, and **Mangrove Seeds** for revitalization projects.

- **Dynamic Environmental System**  
  Experience changing **water levels** and **seasonal cycles** that directly impact infrastructure and economy.

- **Social Trust System**  
  Interact with NPCs such as:
  - *Pak Harjo* (Village Head)  
  - *Mbah Tarjo* (Elder)  
  Their trust influences the village’s future.

- **Multiple Endings**  
  Outcomes depend on your balance between:
  - 🌱 Ecology  
  - 💰 Economy  
  - 🤝 Social Stability
